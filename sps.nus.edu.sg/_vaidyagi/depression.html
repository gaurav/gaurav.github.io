Dealing with Depression

Try:

  1. Check out my Quotes page. I'm a pretty depressive guy, so I try to collect anti-depressivy kinda stuff.
  2. Search the `net for poems. Check out these Robert Frost poems - or even better, try check out this one.
  3. Go onto Yahoo! Messenger (NOT Gaim, which is almost as good - AND free, both as in beer and speech!). Find a friend (force them to download Messenger if you have to, if friends aren't around when you need 'em what are they good for?). Put on voice chat. Load the Doodle IMvironment (click the IMV... button and figure it out, dumbo). Loose yourself :).
  4. Bug people about it. 2x=)
  5. Hang out with loose friends, and loose yourself. Hang out with tight friends, and let them know.
  6. Visit Teen Advice Online. See what's happening in other people's life. Try to make a difference.
  7. Take a walk. Better yet, call a friend and walk with them.
  8. Sit back in a chair, look out the window, stare at the clouds. Slow down your mind until you are moving as slowly as those clouds are.
  9. Read my verse page. Sing along with the more jolly numbers. Start with 'Loverly'! =)
  10. Try some truth. Maybe this will wake you up.
  11. Read Ferris Bueller's Day Off - just to remind yourself that life (properly lived) can be worth it.
  12. Read The Gift of the Magi by O.Henry (one of the best writers EVER!!!!). It's short, it's sweet, it's obvious (ah, well - can the Master perform every time?) but ... it's cool. For a laugh, you might try The Hypotheses Of Failure.
  13. Read Scene 8 of Monty Python's, "The Life of Brian" - commonly known as 'Romanes Eunt Domus' ...
  14. Read this out to yourself ... but ... really ... really ... slowly ...

What to do if a friend is depressed

  1. Sometimes the best thing to do is to simply do nothing. Just be there for them. Sit with them and say nothing. Let them work thru whatever's bugging them; all you've got to give is support. Cos sometimes it's enough.
